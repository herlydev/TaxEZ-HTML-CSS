Welcome to Tax-EZ!

Over 15 yeas ago TaxEZ started with desire to fill a need to provide honest, reliable, and affordable tax preparation services. We are locally and family owned which allows us to adapt to our customers needs making our clients the focus of our business. We continually seek for ways to innovate and incorporate tools to make the experience of tax preparation as complete as possible for our clients.

We believe these four main pillars have made Tax-EZ gain the trust from our customers, year after year:
1. FRIENDLY AND HONEST SERVICE: Our culture is based on providing our customers the best experience in a friendly, open, and honest service.
2. TECHNOLOGY IS IN OUR DNA: Tax-EZ is backed by current technology providing encripted-secured document's portal, online appointment's calendar, and full-virtual-office for remote tax preparation.
3. 15+ YEARS EXPERIENCE SERVING UTAH: TaxEZ provides you the peace of mind of years of experience and latest-tax-code training. Our tax specialists have years in the industry having worked for known companies such H&R Block, Tax Hawk and others.
4. WE SPEAK YOUR LANGUAGE AND UNDERSTAND YOUR CULTURE: Communication is key for Tax-EZ. We currently can serve you in English, Spanish, Portuguese, and Tagalog.
